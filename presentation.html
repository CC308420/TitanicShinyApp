Introduction

This shiny app allow you to predict the chance of survival of passengers on the Titanic.

Dataset

This shiny app use the dataset from library effects

How to Use

Input the following Passenger Info and the predicted result will update automatically.

  • Passenger Class
  • Gender
  • Age

Moreover, you can check out the brief history of the Titanic as well as the figure of the Survival of passengers on the Titanic.
